Industry 4.0

In the past , manufacturers relies on scaling up their business through traditional selling channels. Not only it's painfully slow but expensive to run it global, and it might not be efficient in certain regions.

Majority of the profits saving are obtained through mass production. However, these savings are always eaten away by unforeseen production downtime, inventory shortages, inventory storage cost, shipping cost and increasing labor cost.

Industry 4.0 is about predictive manufacturing. From Big Data analysis, manufacturers can determine when to produce what, when to perform maintenance to minimize unexpected downtime, optimize the process increasing productivity and achieving high yield rate at the same time. And many more....

COVID Virus Detection System

A demonstration of a python program developed to address how Corona Virus could be detected. Everyone knew very well that the field of view under a high magnification microscope is very tiny. A one inch square could take a huge amount of patient and energy to screen through thoroughly. Not only it's time consuming , it is very tiring to the medical staffs too. Not to forget they have to do this with their full protected gear as well. With the amount of suspects rising in thousands by day, it is almost impossible to race against it as every second means someone out there could be transmitted. This is a program developed using Python which ideally to pass on to the next step for Big Data analysis in terms of tracking and traceability. This program might not be the final version since it will still require to integrate with some automated high magnification microscope system and probably my covid19 picture is not enough to represent the actual samples. However, this is a starting point towards working with someone out there to see this solution completed to help our Heroes at the frontlines.
